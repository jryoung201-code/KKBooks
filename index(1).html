Features
Everything you need
to create visual books

Built for manga creators, comic artists, and visual storytellers.

🖼️

Image-based pages

Import PNG, JPG, WEBP. Auto-converts for optimal display. Drag & drop for fast workflows.

📑

Chapter system

Mark chapter starts, add chapter images, navigate with a full chapter manager. .kkBook only.

🔍

Zoom & fullscreen

Zoom with mouse wheel or keyboard. Fullscreen reader mode. Click-drag to pan when zoomed in.

📚

Book library

Browse all your books in a cover grid. Search by title. One click to open any book.

↔️

RTL / LTR support

Full right-to-left for manga. Flips page spread order automatically. .kkBook only.

⠿

Drag-drop reorder

Drag thumbnails to reorder pages. Right-click for set as cover, move, or delete.
